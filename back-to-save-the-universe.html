Description

Arena is a hand painted (with acrylic paint) gladitoral first-person shooter that features an original skybird soundtrack.

It is short and sweet,
clocking in at 15 enemy waves + 1 boss
the game focuses on difficulty, speed running, and a mastery of the different spells.

Inspired by DOOM and Devil Daggers, Arena is designed to get your heart racing!

Features
Real Paint

All of the textures and sprite animations are painted with aryclic


16 Levels

Arena features 15 waves of enemies and 1 boss fight


4 Unique spells

Each of the 4 spells have pros and cons. Knowing when to use what is the key to survival


9 Enemy Types

Battle against the King's finest warriors, goblins, necromancers, and other fearsome baddies


Jump, Block, and Roll

Using your shield costs magic. Dodging enemies costs stamina. Learn the dance to surivive


Speed Running

Compete with the fastest wizards in the world on the global leaderboards


Steam Achievements

Boast of your acheivements as you become the ultimate wizard


Earn New Spells

Collect coins in the arena to buy new spells between rounds


Brief Story

For many ages, magic was banned in the land of Kazu. Our story begins when you (an unnamed wizard) are caught practicing magic in wild.
-
The king of Kazu is disgusted by your magic and sentences you trial by combat. You're afraid, but you are strong. And you will fight for your freedom to exist as your magical self.
